Why does my honey look crystallized or solid?

Crystallization is completely natural and a sign of pure, raw honey. Real honey contains natural sugars that crystallize over time. If your honey never crystallizes — question its purity. To soften it, place the jar in warm (not hot) water for a few minutes.

add
What does "Mono-Floral" mean?

Mono-floral means bees primarily collected nectar from one specific type of flower — Tulsi, Karanj, or Moringa. This gives each honey a distinct, natural flavour profile and unique health properties without any artificial additions.

add
What is Bilona Ghee?

Bilona is the traditional Indian method of making ghee. Instead of melting cream, we turn A2 milk into curd, hand-churn it with a wooden churner to extract butter, then slow-heat it over a low flame. Labor-intensive, but it results in superior nutrition, richer taste, and genuine heritage.
