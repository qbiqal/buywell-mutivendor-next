Frequently Asked Questions

help_outline Why does my honey look crystallized or solid?

Crystallization is a natural process and a sign of pure, raw honey! Real honey contains natural sugars that crystallize over time. If your honey never crystallizes, you should question its purity. To soften it, gently place the jar in warm water.

help_outline What does "Mono-Floral" mean?

Mono-floral means the bees primarily collected nectar from one specific type of flower (like Tulsi, Karanj, or Moringa). This gives each honey a distinct, natural flavor profile without any added artificial flavors.

help_outline What is Bilona Ghee?

Bilona is the traditional Indian method of making ghee. Instead of just melting cream, we turn A2 milk into curd, hand-churn it with a wooden churner to extract butter (makkhan), and then slow-heat it. It's labor-intensive but results in superior nutrition and taste.
